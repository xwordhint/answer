About xwordhint

Our Mission

xwordhint is dedicated to helping crossword enthusiasts enjoy The New York Times crossword puzzles without giving away the answers. We believe that the joy of solving comes from the "aha!" moment, not from being told the solution.

What We Offer

  • Thoughtful Hints: We provide clever nudges and alternative clue interpretations to help you think through challenging answers
  • Solving Strategies: Learn techniques and patterns that will improve your crossword-solving skills
  • No Spoilers: Our hints guide your thinking without revealing answers directly
  • Daily Updates: Fresh hints for each day's NYT crossword puzzle

How to Use Our Hints

Our hints are designed to be progressive - start with the gentlest nudge and work your way up if you need more help. We encourage you to:

  • Try solving on your own first
  • Use hints sparingly to maintain the challenge
  • Focus on learning patterns and techniques for future puzzles

Disclaimer
